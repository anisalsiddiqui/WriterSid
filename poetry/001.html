Tu Chahat hi nahi...

By Anis Al Siddiqui

Tu Chahat hi nahi...

Tu chahat hi nahi, maqsad hai mera
Tu safar hi nahi, manzil hai mera

Mera tasawwur bhi tu, meri haqeeqat bhi tu
Mera kalaam bhi tu, meri qalam bhi tu

Tu khyalon se khwabon tak
Tu lafzon se ash'aaron tak

Main lamhe se zamaane tak,
Main ajnabee se deewane tak

Main Siddiqui,
Mera ishq har paimane tak
