Khwabon mein tujh ko...

By Anis Al Siddiqui

Khwabon mein tujh ko...

Khwabon mein tujhko, Dhundhun main rahbar,
Hai ishq mujhe ya sahr tera hai,
Ab jo bhi ho jaane jaan rahne de,
Yunhi teri yaadon mein jeena hai.

Aa mujhe tu gale se lagaa le,
Hai ishq agar to zara sa dikha de,
Hamein na aaye tu hi sikha de,
Kaise bhala yeh ishq karna hai,

Saanson mein meri hai teri hi khushboo,
Hai ishq mujhe ya koi nasha hai,
Kehti kya aankhein kehne de,
Yunhi mujhko sunte rahna hai.
